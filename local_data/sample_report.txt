Q1 2026 Performance Report — Acme Corp

Executive Summary
-----------------
Q1 2026 saw strong revenue growth of 18% year-over-year, driven primarily by the expansion
of our SaaS product line and a successful launch in the APAC region. Operating costs
increased by only 7%, resulting in an improved EBITDA margin of 24% (up from 19% in Q1 2025).

Key Highlights
--------------
- Total Revenue:        $4.2M  (Q1 2025: $3.56M)
- Gross Profit:         $2.9M  (Gross Margin: 69%)
- Operating Expenses:   $1.9M
- EBITDA:               $1.0M  (Margin: 24%)
- New Customers:        312    (up 40% QoQ)
- Customer Churn Rate:  2.1%   (industry avg: 5.4%)

Product Performance
-------------------
- AI Assistant Module:  $1.8M revenue, best-selling SKU for the third quarter in a row.
- Data Pipeline Pro:    $1.1M revenue, 22% growth following v3.0 release.
- Legacy Suite:         $1.3M revenue, stable but flat — migration campaign planned for Q2.

Regional Breakdown
------------------
- North America:  52% of revenue ($2.18M)
- Europe:         31% of revenue ($1.30M)
- APAC (new):     17% of revenue ($0.72M) — exceeded launch target by 43%

Risks & Outlook
---------------
1. Talent: Engineering headcount is 15% below plan; two senior hires are in final rounds.
2. Competition: Two new entrants in the AI assistant market observed in Q1.
3. Guidance: Q2 2026 revenue forecast is $4.6M–$4.9M, contingent on APAC retention.
